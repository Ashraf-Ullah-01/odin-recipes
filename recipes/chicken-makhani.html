Chicken Makhani (Indian Butter Chicken)

How to Make Chicken Makhani

Chicken Makhani, also known as Butter Chicken, is a rich and creamy Indian dish that's beloved worldwide. Tender chicken is simmered in a spiced tomato and butter sauce, making it perfect to pair with naan or rice. Here's how to make it:

Chicken Makhani Ingredients

To create this flavorful dish, gather the following ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken (preferably thighs), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Plain yogurt (for marinating)
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Ground spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, coriander)
  • Butter
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • Salt to taste

How to Make Chicken Makhani Step-By-Step

Follow these steps for a delicious homemade Butter Chicken:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, garlic-ginger paste, and spices for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until slightly charred and cooked through.
  3. In a large pan, melt butter and sauté onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute, then add tomato puree and spices. Cook until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate.
  5. Add cream and cooked chicken pieces to the sauce. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the chicken absorbs the flavors.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a drizzle of cream before serving.
